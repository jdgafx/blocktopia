⚡ The Story of Blocktopia ⚡

The Great Block Storm scattered everyone across the land. Mira lost her house. Old Pete's mine caved in. Even the fire dinosaurs are grumpy about it.

You are the new Builder — the one who can put the world back together, block by block. The people of Blocktopia will tell you what they need. Start with Mira the Builder — she's waiting in the meadow.

Your first quests are on the right. Double-tap SPACE to fly!
